# Content sourcing, licensing & attribution

Every `content_item` / `media_asset` **must** record its `license` and `attribution`.

## Safe, redistributable sources (seed library)

- **IMSLP** — public-domain scores (verify per-file status per jurisdiction).
- **Musopen** — Public Domain + Creative Commons scores and recordings (PDF / MIDI / LilyPond).
- **Mutopia Project** — public-domain, re-engraved editions (PDF + LilyPond).
- **Hanon / Czerny** and classic technique books — public domain.
- **Open Music Theory (OMT)** — CC BY-SA 4.0 (attribution + share-alike; derivatives inherit the license).

## Caution

- **Jazz-standard melodies are mostly still in copyright.** For play-along/lead-sheets, generate
  from **chord progressions** (not copyrightable) and user-editable charts; only host PD/cleared tunes.
- **Community sheet music** (e.g. MuseScore) is mixed-license — only ingest items explicitly PD/CC.
- Educational lesson sites are free to **learn from and link to**, not to copy.
