Baroque keyboard music teaches independence of the hands like nothing else. This course moves from a
singable minuet to the crystalline counterpoint of the Preludes and Inventions, building the finger
independence and clear articulation the style demands.

## Outcomes
- Voice two independent lines with clear articulation
- Shape Baroque phrases without a sustain pedal
- Understand simple imitative counterpoint

## Section: Warming to the Style
- bach-minuet-in-g (note: Detached left hand, singing right — the Baroque default touch. skills: [articulation])

## Section: Preludes
- bach-prelude-c-major-bwv-846 (note: Keep the broken-chord figuration perfectly even. skills: [evenness, voicing])

## Section: Two-Part Counterpoint
- bach-invention-no1-bwv772 (note: Practise each hand alone until the subject is memorised. skills: [counterpoint, hand-independence])
