Notes get the attention, but rhythm is what makes people move. This path builds your internal clock, then
puts it to work laying down bass lines and strumming grooves. Drill the timing with the
[metronome](/tools/metronome) and the [rhythm tap game](/tools/rhythm-game) as you go.

## Outcomes
- Read and feel note values and meter
- Play steady root-and-fifth and walking bass lines
- Keep a consistent strumming groove
