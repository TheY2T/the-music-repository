Impressionist piano is about sound as colour. Here the pedal is a paintbrush and balance is everything.
Start with Satie's stillness, then move into Debussy's shimmering layers of resonance.

## Outcomes
- Blend and change the pedal for colour without blur
- Balance melody, bass and inner voices
- Shape long, unhurried lines

## Section: Stillness
- satie-gymnopedie-no1 (note: Slow, spacious, and perfectly even. skills: [pedaling, tone])
- satie-gnossienne-no1 (note: Free, unbarred phrasing — breathe with the line.)

## Section: Shimmer
- debussy-arabesque-no1 (note: Flowing triplets over duplets; keep it liquid. skills: [layering])
- debussy-clair-de-lune (note: The classic — voice the melody above a soft, pedalled wash. skills: [voicing, pedaling])
