Solo fingerstyle guitar rests on a handful of classic studies. This path builds right-hand control from
Carulli and Sor through to the singing melodies of Tárrega — the foundation of the classical repertoire.

## Outcomes
- Play clean right-hand arpeggio patterns (p-i-m-a)
- Separate a melody from its accompaniment
- Produce a warm, even tone

## Section: Right-Hand Basics
- carulli-guitar-study (note: Simple arpeggios — anchor the right hand and relax. skills: [arpeggios])
- carcassi-guitar-study-op60-no1 (note: The classic first Carcassi study. skills: [arpeggios, tone])

## Section: Melody & Accompaniment

## Section: The Singing Line
- tarrega-lagrima (note: Tárrega's tender miniature — pure tone and legato. skills: [tone])
- tarrega-adelita (note: A mazurka in miniature; savour the rubato.)
