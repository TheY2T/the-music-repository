Technique on the guitar is built one small piece at a time. These short études each isolate a single
skill — a steady arpeggio, an alternating fingerpicking bass, a Spanish cadence, a singing melody — so
you can master it in miniature before meeting it in the full repertoire. They span from a one-line first
tune to intermediate fingerstyle.

## Outcomes
- Play an even, flowing right-hand arpeggio
- Keep an independent alternating-bass thumb while the fingers play melody
- Shape a two-voice texture and a Spanish (Phrygian) cadence
