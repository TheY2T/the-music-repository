The Romantic miniature is where you learn to sing at the piano — to shape a melody, breathe with rubato,
and colour the sound with the pedal. These short character pieces reward patience and a good ear.

## Outcomes
- Voice a cantabile melody over accompaniment
- Use the sustain pedal for colour, not blur
- Shape phrases with tasteful rubato
