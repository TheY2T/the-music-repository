Triads get you started; seventh chords and the moves between them are where jazz, blues and ragtime get
their flavour. This course builds that harmonic vocabulary and shows it at work in real repertoire. Try
each idea by ear with the [voice-leading viewer](/tools/voice-leading) and the
[chord analyzer](/tools/analyzer).

## Outcomes
- Spell and hear seventh chords of every quality
- Use secondary dominants and borrowed chords
- Recognise these moves in ragtime and blues
