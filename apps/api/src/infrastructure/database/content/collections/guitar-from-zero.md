Everyone starts somewhere. This path builds the right-hand control and reading you need through short,
friendly studies, then rewards you with a well-loved tune to play all the way through.

## Outcomes
- Pluck clean single notes and simple arpeggios
- Read basic notation on the guitar
- Play a complete beginner piece
