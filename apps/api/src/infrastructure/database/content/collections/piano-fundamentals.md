Before the great repertoire comes the groundwork: even scales, independent fingers, and a first taste of
real pieces. This course pairs the essential drills with the tunes that put them to use.

## Outcomes
- Play a two-octave major scale evenly
- Build finger strength and independence
- Perform two complete beginner pieces

## Section: Technique
- c-major-scale-two-octaves (note: The reference scale — master the thumb-under. skills: [scales])
- czerny-op-599-no-1 (note: A tiny study to warm up with daily.)
- hanon-virtuoso-pianist-no-1 (note: Slow and relaxed builds the most strength. skills: [finger-independence])

## Section: First Repertoire
- bach-minuet-in-g (note: Detached left hand, singing right. skills: [reading])
- beethoven-fur-elise-opening (note: The famous opening — mind the pedal. skills: [pedaling])
