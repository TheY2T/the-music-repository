The ukulele is the friendliest instrument to start on — four strings, soft nylon, and songs within reach
in an afternoon. This path takes you from your first chords to strumming and picking real tunes.

## Outcomes
- Fret and change four essential chords cleanly
- Keep a steady strumming pattern
- Play a simple fingerpicked melody
