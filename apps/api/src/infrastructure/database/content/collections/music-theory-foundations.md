Theory makes everything else easier — you stop memorising and start understanding. This syllabus builds
in order: the raw materials (intervals, scales, rhythm), then how chords are built, then how they move.

## Outcomes
- Identify intervals and build any major scale
- Spell triads and seventh chords in any key
- Recognise common cadences and chord functions

## Section: The Raw Materials
- omt-intervals (note: Everything in harmony is measured in intervals — start here. skills: [ear-training])
- omt-major-scale-key-signatures
- omt-rhythm-and-meter

## Section: Building Chords
- omt-triads-and-inversions (note: Triads are the atoms of harmony.)
- omt-diatonic-chords (note: The seven chords every key gives you for free.)
- omt-seventh-chords

## Section: How Harmony Moves
- omt-cadences (note: Cadences are harmony's punctuation.)
- omt-voice-leading (note: Smooth voice leading is what makes progressions sound good. skills: [analysis])
